# Hard Constraints — La Rutina Diaria (9th Grade, ACTFL Novice Low)

These are non-negotiable requirements for all three stages of this lesson.
Before generating or revising any content, verify the task does not violate any constraint below.
If a revision request conflicts with a constraint, stop and report the conflict — do not proceed.

---

## 1. Tense
- **Present tense only.**
- No past tenses (preterite, imperfecto, present perfect) may appear anywhere — not in instructions, sentence frames, model examples, rubric descriptors, or student-facing scaffolds.
- This applies to all three stages (pre-task, main activity, reflective loop).

## 2. Grammar Structures (exactly two)
- **Structure 1:** Reflexive verbs in the present tense (yo, tú, él/ella forms).
- **Structure 2:** Frequency adverbs — siempre, casi siempre, normalmente, a veces, casi nunca, nunca.
- No additional grammar structures may be introduced or assessed.

## 3. Permitted Vocabulary Set (PVS)
The following 15 items are the only Spanish content vocabulary allowed. No additional Spanish vocabulary may be introduced in any exercise, frame, model sentence, or example.

1. acostarse
2. bañarse
3. cepillarse el pelo
4. cepillarse los dientes
5. despertarse
6. dormirse
7. lavarse la cara
8. lavarse las manos
9. levantarse
10. maquillarse
11. peinarse
12. ponerse
13. quitarse
14. sentarse
15. vestirse

Function words, prepositions, conjunctions, and the six frequency adverbs listed above are not PVS-regulated.

## 4. Proficiency Level
- **ACTFL Novice Low throughout.**
- All production tasks must provide sentence frames, on-page vocabulary chips, and completion-based rubrics.
- Accuracy (spelling, accent marks, agreement) must not be scored.

## 5. Grade Level
- **9th grade.**
- Scenarios, topics, and examples must be appropriate and relevant for 9th-grade students.

## 6. Theme
- **Daily activities at home and at school.**
- All tasks, scenarios, and complications must stay within this theme.

## 7. Constraint Check Procedure (for orchestrator and specialist agents)
Before generating or revising any content:
1. Read this file.
2. List any constraint that the requested task or revision could violate.
3. If a violation is found, report it explicitly and halt — do not generate the violating content.
4. Only proceed once the violation is resolved by the teacher.
